Exploring the worlds we Love

Every world worth getting lost in is built from thousands of small decisions, lore that ties back further than you'd expect, details placed on purpose, stories hiding in plain sight. Geekotist exists to surface those pieces and share them properly: not as trivia, but as the reason these worlds get under your skin in the first place. At its core, this is a celebration. Of the writers, designers, and artists who put in the work. Of the worlds they built. And of the audience who noticed enough to go looking for more.
