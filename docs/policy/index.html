Cookie Policy

Learn more about how and why we utilize cookies on our system to deliver a smooth navigation experience.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small files stored directly on your computer's hard drive by your web browser. They help websites remember preferences and details about your past visits.

2. Our Cookie Implementations

We utilize cookies to store persistent display filters (e.g. accordion states or theme selections) so you don't have to reconfigure them each time you load the homepage.

3. Disabling Cookies

You can adjust your browser configurations to decline all cookies or to alert you when a cookie is placed. Note that doing so may reset certain layout preferences upon page refreshes.
